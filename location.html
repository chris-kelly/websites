Symondsdown House is easy to find – just off the A35 on the Axminster/Lyme Regis road.

Come off the A35 towards Axminster, take the first lane on the left, Woodbury Lane, follow it, go around a sharp right hand bend, past a number of cottages, and the entrance to Symondsdown House can be found 100 yards on the left. If you get as far as the Church on your right, you have just missed us!

The gates may be closed because of the dogs but don’t be put off, come through them and meet us at the front door.
