    OUR ETHOS
Here at DassenVanDassen, we believe that creativity will always be a cut above the catwalk, and if you want something done well, then you should do it yourself.

Creating unique crochet garments is our passion, and we want to share it with you. With our innovative designs and intuitive new tutorial process, we will have you making your own bespoke garments in no time.
JOANNA DASSEN - DESIGNER
Customisation
Regardless of your shape or size, you can create your own tailor-made garments using our system of online tutorials.

Creativity
With the ability to control every factor, you can personalise each garment to your own satisfaction. Become the designer, forget the high street and be your own trendsetter.
