 		 Holiday Cottages
The Peach House and Orangery are both modern buildings that provide 4-star accommodation while retaining much character and charm.

Situated in a secluded rural location in the seven acres of grounds that surround Symondsdown House, these properties have private use of the adjoining secure 4-acre field – ideal for exercising dogs.

Both properties are equipped to a high standard and have been designed with no internal or external steps. The facilities include:
  • Open plan kitchen/diner with electric hob/oven
  • Fridge with icebox
  • Microwave
  • Dishwasher
  • Electric panel heating
  • Lounge area with TV/DVD/CD
  • Bedroom with a king-size bed (or 2' 6'' twin beds) made up on arrival
  • Shower room/wet room with towels provided
  • Access to the utility room in the main house with a washing machine and tumble dryer
  • Off-road parking for up to two cars
Rates vary according to season and availability. Please contact Stuart or Marina for further information.
