About Us
  
Stuart and Marina fell in love with Symondsdown House and decided to move from London to Devon for a complete life style change. They are now offering to share this beautiful property with others by opening up some of the rooms, the cottages, and even, on occasions if requested, the whole house!

Their aim is to provide houseguests with the experience of living in a charming setting whilst feeling completely relaxed and ‘at home’.

They pride themselves in being able to guide their guests towards the delights of Devon, Dorset and Somerset, enabling them to make the best of their time exploring this stunning part of the world.

This includes being able to recommend:
  • Places to visit – towns, villages, beaches
  • Local events – fetes, festivals, markets, auctions
  • Places to shop – butchers, farm shops, wineries
  • Places to eat/drink – pubs, restaurants
With their dogs, Harry and Bertie (pictured right) - and cats, Paddy and Sunny - Symondsdown House is a pet-friendly environment that allows pets in most, but not all, parts of the property.
