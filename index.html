Symondsdown House
Stuart and Marina welcome you to share their dog friendly home, Symondsdown House. Nestled in seven acres of private grounds, this stunning Georgian house and two self-contained cottages are situated just outside the bustling market town of Axminster. The house commands an elevated yet sheltered location, enjoying far reaching views over the Axe valley and beyond. The countryside is as dramatic as it is unspoilt and is designated as an Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty with the Jurassic Coast and Lyme Regis resort just a few miles away.
 
 
  
 


Bed & Breakfast

Enjoy the ambience of this beautiful house and the warm hospitality of its hosts - Stuart and Marina. All the rooms are furnished to a high standard and have en-suite facilities.


Holiday Cottages

The Orangery and The Peach House are two cottages situated in the surrounding seven acres of grounds. They provide a tranquil base from which to explore the stunning coast and countryside.


Bespoke Retreats

Make use of the splendid facilities - the accommodation, the grounds, and the setting - to tailor a retreat to suit your requirements.
