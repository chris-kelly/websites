Bed & Breakfast
Symondsdown House has eight bedrooms two of which have been designated and tailored for Bed & Breakfast guests. Both rooms are equipped with wi-fi access, smart TVs, and tea & coffee making facilities. Toiletries are provided in the shower rooms.
 
 
      Deluxe Double
The Deluxe Double has a south-facing perspective and is equipped with furniture that is in keeping with the style of the house. It has an en-suite shower room that has a bright south-facing outlook. Pets can be accommodated in this room, subject to a surcharge dependant on size.

The Deluxe Room is available for two people for £109 per night. 		   
 
             
 
             
      Superior Double
The Superior Double is an extremely spacious and light room that has north and east facing perspectives. As with the Deluxe Double, the furniture is in keeping with the style of the house. It has an en-suite shower room that has an east-facing outlook. This room is kept free of pets.

The Superior Room is available for two people for £129 per night.
   
             
                 
Other rooms are available by special arrangement. In particular, there is a large spacious family room that also accommodates pets.

A full English breakfast is prepared in the kitchen and presented in the Morning Room.

Guests are encouraged to enjoy the house and surroundings of our beautiful home and have access to the main rooms in the house including the Sitting Room and Dining Room both of which are pet free.

In order to take full advantage of the surrounding areas, lifts to Axminster or Lyme Regis may be available at additional cost.
